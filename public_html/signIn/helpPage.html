Help Page

  • What is Lightbulb?

    Lightbulb is a social media site that has forum elements in the form of comment threads for posts. Users can log-in to any account as long as they have the correct username, so please note that the website is not secure and anyone can log-in to any user!

  • How do I sign-up?

    To sign-up, just click on the sign up button on the login screen. This will re-direct you to a page where you can specify a username (unchangeable once set!) and a display name, which displays to other users on your profile.

  • How do I log-in?

    To log-in, make sure to first register an account on the site using the sign up button on the login page. Then, simply enter your username into the Username box, and hit login.

  • How do I edit my profile?

    To edit your profile, first log-in to your user account. Then, on the top right of the screen, click account. You will be re-directed to the account page. Then, edit the info boxes on the right side of the page, and hit submit. This will edit your profile info, which updates the boxes on the left side of the page accordingly.

  • How do I search for other users on the site?

    To search for other users on the site, just type in the name of the user whom you wish to add, and hit enter. To find a more general search of users, type a period "." into the search bar, and hit enter.

  • How do I follow people?

    To follow people, search for a user in the search bar. When the results finish loading, you will be able to follow/unfollow any user you wish by hitting the "follow" or "unfollow" button next to the user's name.

  • How do I create posts?

    To make a new post, you must first be logged-in. Then, click on the "+" icon in the top navigation bar. Once redirected, you will be able to type the content of the post in the text box displayed. Once you are ready to submit your post, click the "post" button. This will make your post visible to all others on the website. Please note that posts can not be deleted once made.

  • How do I view my own posts?

    To view your own posts. Click on Account on the top right next to your display name. Once your on the account page, press the “view your profile” button. And this will take you to your profile page where you can see all your posts.

  • How do I comment on posts?

    To comment on a post, first find a post to comment on. You can locate a comment by clicking on any users name, which directs to their profile page. Or, if you follow other users, you will have a feed of posts on your home page as well. Once you have found a post to comment on, click "see comment section". Once in this section, write your comment in the comment section, and click "post comment". Please note that comments can not be deleted once made.

  • How do I like and dislike posts?

    To like or dislike a post, first find a post which you want to interact with from either the feed on the main page or a user's profile page. Simply click on the thumbs up or thumbs down icons to like or dislike a post as many times as you wish.

  • How do I signout?

    To sign out, first click the the home button on the navigation bar at the top-left of the screen, denoted by a home icon. Then, click account in the top right of the screen. Once redirected, click signout in the top right of the screen to signout.
